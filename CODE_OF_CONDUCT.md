# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1][covenant] as its Code of
Conduct. By participating, you agree to uphold these expectations in all
project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions).

## Summary

- Be respectful and constructive. Disagree with ideas, not people.
- Assume good faith. Ask before escalating.
- No harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Keep discussions focused on the work; take heated side threads elsewhere.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, email
**tjdhkdrb@gmail.com** with details. Reports are kept confidential and
acted on in good faith.

[covenant]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
